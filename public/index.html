SAPS: About

ABSTRACT:

The study developed the “SMART GARDEN AND DATA ANALYTICS/MONITORING FOR PLANT SUSTAINABILITY (SAPS)”. The objectives of the study are to create an IOT device that allows a user to monitor the status of the plant being raised; monitor the temperature and humidity of the surroundings, and to keep track of the moisture of the soil. The system was developed using Raspberry Pi.

About the Initiative:

The aim of this research is to develop an all-around monitoring and solution for Plant Enthusiasts (Plantito/Plantita) that will monitor REAL-TIME data about its surroundings, and irrigate the plants when it needs it.
